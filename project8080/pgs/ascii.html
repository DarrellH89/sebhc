H-19 Graphics Characters

    Turn on graphics mode by sending <esc>F. Turn off graphics mode by sending <esc>G.

    The graphics characters correspond to the characters from 136Q ("^") through 176Q ("~") inclusive. Ref: H-19 Programming Information, Modes of Operation for more information.
